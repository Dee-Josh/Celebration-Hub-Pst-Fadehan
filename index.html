Adesola Fadehan serves as the esteemed senior pastor of Eagles Christian Chapel International, a thriving ministry nestled in the vibrant community of Baruwa Ipaja, Lagos. With a background in mechanical engineering, Pastor Fadehan is not only dedicated to spiritual leadership but also brings a wealth of knowledge from his professional career.

He is widely recognized as the author of the influential work, "Destroying Satanic Strategies". Beyond his ministry and literary contributions, Pastor Fadehan finds profound joy in his marriage to the cherished Olorunkemi Fadehan. Together, they are blessed with three wonderful children: Pipeloluwa, David, and Daniel.
